Common Questions

How do I cancel Premium?
Manage subscriptions in the Apple App Store under your Apple ID.

How do I delete a document?
Open the document dashboard, tap the document, and choose delete.

Is my content private?
Yes. Documents are processed securely to generate study materials and are not sold or used for advertising.
