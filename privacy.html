Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Overview

OmniStudy helps you transform documents into quizzes, flashcards, summaries, and insights. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the OmniStudy iOS app and this website.

Information We Collect

We value privacy, so we do not collect any user data. Your documents and study activity stay on your device.

How We Use Information

We use your information to generate study content, provide support, personalize your learning experience, and improve the app. Documents are processed securely and only used to deliver the features you request.

Document Processing

Documents are processed on-device to create quizzes, flashcards, and summaries. Content never leaves your device and is not stored on our backend.

Data Retention

We do not retain any user data ever. All content is stored locally on your device and is never stored in our backend.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data in transit and at rest. No system is perfectly secure, but we continuously improve safeguards and access controls.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers necessary to run the app, such as hosting, analytics, and AI processing services. These providers are required to protect your information.
