# Instructions for OmniStudy Landing Website

Please design and build a modern, high-converting landing page for the **OmniStudy** iOS app (formerly Doc2Quiz).

## 1. Project Overview
**App Name**: OmniStudy
**Tagline**: Turn Any Document into a Study Masterclass.
**Core Value**: Instantly transform study materials (PDFs, notes) into interactive quizzes, flashcards, summaries, and deep insights using AI.
**Target Audience**: Students, lifelong learners, and professionals preparing for exams or needing to master content quickly.
**Platform**: iOS (iPhone) only.

## 2. Design Aesthetic
- **Style**: Clean, modern, and academic yet vibrant. Think "Apple Design" meets "Headspace" or "Notion".
- **Color Palette**: Use the app's accent color (a vibrant blue/purple gradient or similar) against a clean white/dark mode background. Glassmorphism for cards.
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif fonts (Inter, SF Pro Display).
- **Responsiveness**: Mobile-first design, as the user will likely visit from their phone.

## 3. Key Sections Required

### A. Hero Section
- **Headline**: "Master Your Study Material in Seconds."
- **Subheadline**: "Upload your PDFs or notes. OmniStudy automatically generates quizzes, flashcards, and summaries to help you learn faster."
- **Visual**: A high-quality mockup of an iPhone running the app, showing the **Document Dashboard** or the **Quiz Interface**.
- **CTA**: "Download on the App Store" button (Use official Apple badge).

### B. "How It Works" (Simplified Flow)
1.  **Upload**: Import any PDF or text file.
2.  **Analyze**: AI instantly analyzes the full document and generates a learning plan.
3.  **Learn**: Take quizzes, review flashcards, or chat with your document.

### C. Feature Showcase
Highlight these key features with icons and short descriptions:
-   **Instant Onboarding Analysis**: Get a full document breakdown, initial quiz, and study notes the moment you upload.
-   **AI Quizzes**: Customizable quizzes (5-50 questions) with difficulty levels and streak rewards.
-   **Smart Flashcards**: Auto-generated study notes in a beautiful carousel view.
-   **Document Q&A**: Chat with your document to ask specific questions.
-   **Detailed Page Analysis**: Deep dive into complex PDFs page-by-page (Premium feature).
-   **Summaries**: Get concise or detailed summaries of long texts.

### D. Interactive Demo / Video Placeholder
-   Place a placeholder for a 30-60 second demo video showing the app flow: *Import -> Loading Screen (Page Analysis) -> Taking a Quiz*.

### E. Pricing / Tiers (Optional but good for clarity)
-   **Free**: 1 Document limit, basic quizzes.
-   **Premium**: Unlimited documents, advanced AI models (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini), full page analysis, unlimited questions.

## 4. Technical Constraints & Details
-   **Monetization**: Mention "Free to start, upgrade for power features."
-   **Privacy**: Emphasize that documents are processed securely.
-   **Availability**: "Available exclusively on iOS." (Do **not** include Google Play badges).

## 5. Copy Tone
-   Empowering, intelligent, efficient, and encouraging.
-   Avoid technical jargon where possible; focus on "learning faster" and "saving time."
